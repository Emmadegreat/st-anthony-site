Our organizations

CMO

Catholic Men Organization (CMO) is one of the three major organizations in the catholic church. It is an umbrella body for all the married men in the church. CMO St. Anthony Alagbado holds their monthly meeting every 2nd Sunday of the month, immediately after second mass. Slogan: CMO ! Christ is our leader !

See more...

CWO

This body encompasses all the married women in the catholic church. They are the mothers in the house, and the 2nd in command in terms of organizational status. All the married women in the parish are encouraged to identify with our CWO. Monthly meetings holds every 1st Sunday. slogan: CWO ! through Mary we succeed !.

See more...

CYON

Catholic youth organization of Nigeria (CYON), is an association comprising all the young men and ladies that are not married from the age of 15. CYON is known for their vibrant and activeness in the church activities both physical and spiritual. Slogan: For God and for the youth ! through christ !. CYON members of St Anthony Alagbado, holds their monthly meeting on every 3rd Sunday of the month.

Project

We thank God for the wonders He is doing in our parish through His amiable and workaholic servant Very Rev. Fr Christian Echewodo (Ichie 1); who apart from his spiritual endowments have been working tirelessly on different projects. Noteable among them are the newly built gigantic and beautiful Eucharistic adoration chapel, Our lady of Fatima's grotto, parish office, parish convenience and the interlocking of the entire church compound.
The above mentioned projects was built within the space one less than one year. They were all commissioned by His Grace Alfred Adewale Martins, Catholic Archbishop of the Lagos Metropolitian See, on the 7th Of July 2022. To God be the glory!, amen.
